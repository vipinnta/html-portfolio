Dear Arya...

I love you without knowing how,
or when, or from where. I love
you simply, without problems or
pride: I love you in this way
because I do not know any other
way of loving but this, in which
there is no I or you, so intimate
that your hand upon my chest is my
hand, so intimate that when I fall
asleep your eyes close.
